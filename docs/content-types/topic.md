Topic pages serve as central hubs for DVRPC’s core areas of work:

- <a href="https://dev-dvrpc.pantheonsite.io/transportation-planning" target="_blank">Transportation Planning</a>
- Community Planning
- Economic Development
- Environment

Each Topic page brings together related content—including subtopics, programs, news, events, datasets, publications, and mapping tools—into a single, easy-to-navigate location.

### How Content Appears on Topic Pages
Most content on Topic pages is automatically generated based on how items are tagged across the website.

When a [Topic Subpage](../content-types/topic-subpage.md) is assigned a Topic, it will automatically appear on the corresponding Topic page.

Related content (programs, news, events, datasets, publications, and maps & applications) is also pulled in based on these Topic selections.

This ensures Topic pages stay up to date without requiring manual updates.

### What You Can Control
You can manually adjust the following content sections if needed:

- Programs
- News
- Events
- Datasets
- Publications
- Maps & Applications

This allows you to feature or prioritize specific content beyond the automated selections.

### What You Cannot Control
- Topic Subpages are fully automated and cannot be manually overridden.
- These are always displayed based on their assigned Topic.
